“Rhythm is a healing power that combines the above with the below and through whirling breath and pulsating blood constantly creates anew”

Christopher Sivertsen er født i Norge, men bor og jobber I England. Han er en av grunnleggerne i The Awake Project (www.awakeproject.com). I februar kommer han til Norge for å holde en workshop om deres spesielle arbeidsmetode. Slik beskrives tidligere workshoper:

The Breathing Performer workshops will combine this rich body of work with the exciting new research he has been developing as the artistic director of The Awake Project. Since 2007 The Awake Ensemble have been developing an approach to theatre training and performance founded upon an investigation of breath and all aspects of rhythm.

The rhythm of the music:
Beat, tempo, time, pulse, and swing.

The rhythm of language:
Poetic features such as meter, measure, stress, accent, and cadence.

The rhythm of daily life:
Pattern, flow, tempo and duration.

The rhythm of connection:
Timing, distance, directions and listening.
