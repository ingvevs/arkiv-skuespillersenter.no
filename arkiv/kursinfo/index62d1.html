The Way of the Fool is simply and radical surrendering to life, just as it
is. A «fool» was once thought to be a «simple innocent into whom spirit
could easily enter». In other words, to be simple, to be foolish, is to be
connected. These workshops use the archetype of the fool who lives in the
present moment, in touch with emotion and heart.

The workshop helps you to dare to be vulnerable, honest and playful. So
called «failure» becomes a doorway to astonishing creativity and the
ability to notice and use new possibilities presenting themselves
continually. By continually practicing being in the body and staying in
touch with the senses, it is possible to become sensitive, authentic and
more awake to the unfolding of life moment by moment. The actor becomes connected to the vast field of energy and develops presence, compassion and depth.
The workshop is created organically in the present with continuous feedback from the group. Core elements are clown, play, movement, self-inquiry, stillness, meditation. Supporting elements are voice work, writing, yoga- based warm up. All the work is physically based with attention on the breath and a welcoming attitude to emotion.

Angela Halvorsen Bogo discovered improvised theatre 35 years ago. She then
trained at Fooltime Circus School,and with many clown and voice teachers
in the UK and Europe. As a solo storyteller for 22 years with both children and adults in over 20 countries.  She has used the fool archetype
in her own work and inner journey for many years. The Way of the Fool is a
workshop developed for 15 years. She established the Community of Fools in
2010 in the Uk and will bring this to Norway in 2011. For more information
please go ot  www.thefoolstory.com
