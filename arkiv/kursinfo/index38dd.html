Prøverom: I am not deciding med Bookhouse Boys

22.10.2011 til 24.10.2011
Start kl :
17:00

22:00

Norsk Skuespillersenter, Welhavensgate 1, Oslo

   I oktober 2011 benyttet teaterkompaniet Bookhouse Boys, bestående av Maritea Dæhlin og Jo Hellier, Prøverommet til videreutvikling av sin forestilling I Am Not Deciding.

"In October 2011 BOOKHOUSE BOYS had the privilege of using Prøverommet at Norsk Skuespillersenter to develop their performance I Am Not Deciding. Without this opportunity it would have been very difficult for the company to afford any rehearsal space in Oslo, as they were operating on a very small budget.

Norsk Skuespillersenter was a life line for the company as it allowed them time and space to invite an outside eye into their rehearsal process, which helped them greatly with the finishing touches of their performance.

Consequently BOOKHOUSE BOYS performed three nights of performances at Café Teatret, then went on to perform at various venues in the UK. It is vital to support emerging artists so they can develop their practice without having the boundaries that financial pressures can cause, therefore we thank Norsk Skuespillersenter greatly for supporting our company BOOKHOUSE BOYS."
