     Unwrap 2013 Selection Session er avlyst.

ACTS – laboratory for performance practices has an open call for work-in-progress presentations for Unwrap 2013. All professional artists from different creative fields are welcome to apply.

 

Fill out an application form at the ACTS web-page. Selected applicants will be invited to the Selection Session at Skuespillersenter 21th September (artists living outside Oslo or abroad have their selection via Skype).

 

Works must contain:

  • contemporary perspective to performative arts, with the body as main artistic medium
  • experimental approach that merges different artistic expressions
  • max 20 min live presentation at venue and max 12 min live presentation via livestream
  • minimalistic technical requirements

 

Our criteria are:

  • innovation and diversity of the work
  • context and plan for execution of the work
  • specific technical needs

 

We offer:

  • 1 week of free rehearsal space at Torshovloftet
  • stage (with minimalistic light and sound supply)
  • professional feedback and open discussion
  • national and international visibility
  • digital photo / video material of the work presented at Unwrap
  • simple guidance for use of livestream –  if needed

 

 

Unwrap takes place at Cafeteatret 1st November. ACTS will select 2 artists/groups for live presentation at the venue and 1 artist/group - from outside Oslo or Norway - for live presentation via livestream. We prioritize independent artists.

 

Unwrap is an international meeting point for artists, theoreticians and audience to come together for lecture, work presentation and open discussion. Using interactive medias to expand audience and artists visibility.

 

Deadline: 14th September (application form)
