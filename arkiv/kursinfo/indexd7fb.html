Staged reading of «In Oglinda»

Dato:
22.03.2012 til 22.03.2012
Start kl :
16:00
Slutt kl :
17:00
Adresse:
Welhavensgate 1, Oslo

After a four day workshop with  British theatre director Jennifer Lunn, there will be a staged reading of the English translation of In Oglinda by playwright Malmfrid Hallum. 

Working in English, actors have been looking at different interpretations of the play and worked on different ways of staging a performance.

Jennifer Lunn is Artistic Director of Culturcated Theatre in London, producing and directing contemporary takes on new and classic plays. She is also Associate Director at the Jack Studio Thetare in London where she has worked to establish a home for new writing, writer development and script development. She has been working with Malmfrid Hallum for several years on In Oglinda and is working towards a full production in London in the next year.
