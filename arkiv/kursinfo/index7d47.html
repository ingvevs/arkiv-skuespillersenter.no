Den amerikanske skuespilleren Shirley Knight er i Norge for å promotere thrilleren Elevator som har Norgespremiere i januar, og vises ved Tromsø Internasjonale Filmfestival (TIFF). I den forbindelse kommer hun også til Norsk Skuespillersenter for å holde en todagers Masterclass der hun arbeider med scener fra Tre Søstre av Checkhov, som hun selv har spilt i.

Masterclassen vil foregå på engelsk.

Shirley Knight
One of Americas favourite actresses through six decades, Shirley Knight studied acting with Lee Strasberg and is a member of The Actor’s Studio. Her award list is long, including: a Tony Award, a Venice Film Festival Best Actress Award, Cannes Film Festival Critics Prize, three Emmy's, two Golden Globe's, the Evening Standard Award and a New Jersey Drama Critics Award. She has an outstanding two Academy Award Nominations, ten Emmy Award Nominations, three Golden Globe Award Nominations, two Tony Award Nominations, two Drama Desk Nominations, and one British Academy Award Nomination.

She has acted in a huge number of films, including classics like Sweet bird of youth (directed by Richard Brooks) and The rain people (directed by Francis Ford Coppola). She has also appeared in TV-series like House, Law and Order and E.R., and currently has a recurring part in Desperate Housewifes. In the theatre she has appeared in productions of The Three Sisters (directed by Lee Strasberg), The Vagina Monologues, The Cherry Orchard, The Glass Menagerie, A Streetcar Named Desire and Hamlet, amongst others.
