 A workshop in musicality, movement and Shakespeare with Anna-Helena McLean and Wilhelm Støylen.

We will be working through rhythm, breath and song to open the actors exploration of text. Focusing on creating a strong ensemble through shared skills this workshop aims to release the voice of the individual. Anna-Helena and Wilhelm come together for the first time sharing a decade of experience as actors, directors and musicians working with the renowned companies Gardzienice and Song of the Goat from Poland.

Please prepare a short text from Shakespeare that you know by heart - in English or Norwegian (please bring a hard copy of your text in an English translation if you work in Norwegian). Wear clothes that are comfortable for physical activity.

The workshop will be conducted in English.

Wilhelm Støylen har gått i lære hos Song of the goat theatre i Polen og blant anna vore på ekspedisjon til Hellas og opplevd og lært av fleire urgamle kulturar som Anastenaria, gråtekoner, polyfonisk synging, Pontos dansar og Bysantinsk musikk. Han driv teatergruppa Teater Tramp og jobbar som frilans skodespelar og instruktør. Søken etter måtar å lytte med heile kroppen er eit viktig grunnprinsipp i hans arbeide der fysisk uttrykk og musikalitet går hand i hand. 

Wilhelm har erfaring frå teaterkompani som jobbar med skodespelaren i sentrum for kreasjon og som gjer research i teaterteknikk over lengre tid. Han har blant anna trena og/eller jobba med Double Edge Theatre (MA, USA), Odin Teatret (DK), Institut for Scenkonst (Sverige), Artus (Ungarn), Gardzienice (Polen), Song of the goat theatre (Polen), Traslapo Teatro (Mexico), The Working Party (Scotland), Teater Grimsborken (Oslo).

Anna-Helena graduated from Royal Holloway, University of London in Music and Drama. She was a principal member of the world-renowned Polish physical theatre company Gardzienice for over 7 years. Her performances with the company included Metamorphoses and Electra (LaMama New York’ 04; Barbican London ‘06). Founder and Artistic Director of Moon Fool and Actor-Chorus-Text training practice and ACT Youth which tour internationally. Anna-Helena previously worked with Trestle as Vocal and Movement Associate on their production of The Glass Mountain (2009) and recently performed in The Awake Project which showed in Sweden September ‘09. Anna-Helena has been a guest director and trainer for BA and MA actors at the RSAMD since ‘04 and the NSD in Delhi since Jan ‘08.
