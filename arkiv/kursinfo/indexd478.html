This is a two day Masterclass in Frantic Assembly’s unique approach to creating physical material. This celebrated and experienced British company will deliver two energetic days of physical theatre techniques for actors and dancers. The workshop will give an insight into how Frantic Assembly creates new work and provide skills for participants to embark on thier own devised and physical theatre projects.

A typical workshop will include:
• A thorough warm up (approx 30 mins)
• Team building and trust exercises
• Creative tasks and devising processes
• A cool down, reflection and questions

Outcomes:
• Promoting physical confidence and encourage participants to look beyond self perceived limitations within a fun, energetic and safe environment.
• Instructing students on the various devising processes used by Frantic Assembly when working collaboratively to produce multi disciplined performance.
• Addressing key skills required to enhance their abilities in the areas of performance, movement, directing and critical analysis.

Biog of Company
Frantic Assembly is amongst the UK’s most celebrated, accomplished and exciting theatre companies. The company has created innovative, high quality touring productions for 16 years and its acclaimed work has consistently attracted new and young audiences. Frantic Assembly’s influence on the development of contemporary theatre in the UK is significant and was recently acknowledged by the founding artistic directors’, Scott Graham and Steven Hoggett, inclusion in the annual Stage 100 list of the theatre industry’s most important people. Frantic Assembly makes its unique process accessible to the next generation of artists through its Train activities and contributes considerably to young people’s experience of the arts via its extensive Learn activities. Jointly these programmes benefit over 5,000 participants a year.

Frantic Assembly holdt en meget populær workshop på Norsk Skuespillersenter i november 2010, og vi er veldig stolte over at vi i samarbeid med Teater Innlandet nå kan tilby en workshop med dette spennende kompaniet til skuespillere og dansere i innlandsfylkene!

OBS: The workshop will take place in English!
