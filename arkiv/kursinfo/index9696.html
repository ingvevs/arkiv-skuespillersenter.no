Staged reading
«Killer Story» - a new play by American playwright Dan Harder, was presented in an english staged reading/workshop performance Sunday March 10 at 15.00 at the Norsk Skuespillersenter. The play featured performances by Dean Clark, Torgny Aanderaa and Melanie Smirou in the principal roles, with music by Joe LaCasce on the saxophone. "Killer Story" is a poetic murder mystery that employs a unique overlapping structure to the dialouge, creating a fresh and lyrical look at a 1940`s-style murder mystery.  The work examines the power of stories to influence our lives, for better or worse, and the consequences of putting too much faith into a narrative.

After the show there was a session with audience feedback.
