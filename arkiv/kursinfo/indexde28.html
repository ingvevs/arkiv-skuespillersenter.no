   OBS. Vi har dessverre vært nødt til å avlyse masterclassen på grunn av uforutsette omstendigheter.

This masterclass serves as an introduction to actor-musicianship, and shows how live music and musicality can form a conceptual starting point when producing theatre and performance.

Discover the benefits of musicality for an actor and the theatrical possibilities that lie in playing an instrument!

Actor-musician, director and teacher Jeremy Harrison is one of the leading figures in British music-theatre. His workshop at Norsk Skuespillersenter in march inspired the participants (a group of both actors and musicians) to start their own actor-musician project. They are starting laboratory work this autumn, working towards a full scale performance. Find out what inspired them in this one day masterclass!
